The Cocoa Quiz

Are you a fan of chocolate? Whether you prefer milk chocolate or dark chocolate or white chocolate, it all originates with cocoa. How much you know about the world's favorite sweet treat?

Chocolate starts out as a...

  • Hard green fruit on a thorny cocoa vine.
  • Dark liquid found under the bark of the cocoa tree.
  • Soft nut on a short prickly cocoa bush.
  • Tiny seed in a purple pod in a tall cocoa tree.

Fun Fact: Chocolate is made from the cocoa nib found inside the seeds which are inside the pods which dangle from the cocoa trees found only in tropical climates!
