The Cocoa Quiz

Are you a fan of chocolate? Do you know what it's made from? Let's see how much you know about the world's favorite confection...

Cocoa is...

  1. A small town in Florida.
  2. An ingredient required to make chocolate.
  3. Apple’s object­-oriented API for OS X.
  4. All of the above.

#4 is correct. Cocoa is the basic ingredient required to make chocolate. But it takes almost a year for a cocoa tree to produce enough for 10 standard-sized Hershey bars.

Fun Fact: Cocoa comes from the tiny nib inside the cocoa seed(bean). There are 20-50 seeds inside a pod and a cocoa tree produces 5-7 pods annually.
