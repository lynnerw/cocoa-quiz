The Cocoa Quiz

Are you a fan of chocolate? Do you know what it's made from? Let's see how much you know about the world's favorite confection...

Cocoa is...

  • A small town in Florida.
  • An ingredient used to make chocolate.
  • Apple’s object­-oriented API for OS X.
  • All of the above.

Cocoa is the basic ingredient needed to make chocolate. But it takes almost a year for a cocoa tree to produce enough for 10 standard-sized Hershey bars.

Fun Fact: The cocoa we use to make chocolate is from a tiny nib found inside the seeds which are inside the pods which dangle from the cocoa trees that only grow in tropical climates!
